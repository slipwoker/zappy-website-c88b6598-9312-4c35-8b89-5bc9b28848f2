Each piece is entirely handcrafted from natural olive or acacia wood, meaning no two are identical. Wood grain patterns, knots, and the flow of epoxy resin vary organically from piece to piece. These natural variations are not imperfections — they are the signature of genuine handmade craft. The photos show representative examples; your piece will carry its own unique character.

How do I care for my cutting board or mezuzah?

For cutting boards, hand wash with mild soap and warm water — never soak or place in a dishwasher. Dry immediately and apply food-safe mineral oil every few months to nourish the wood and keep the epoxy resin vibrant. For mezuzahs and decorative pieces, simply wipe with a soft dry cloth. Avoid prolonged direct sunlight to preserve the depth of the resin colors.
