Olive Wood & Epoxy Resin 

The Fusion of Nature and Art: Olive Wood & Epoxy Resin
Olive Wood and Epoxy Resin products represent a perfect harmony between ancient tradition and modern innovation. By combining the raw, organic beauty of centuries-old olive trees with the sleek, versatile nature of high-quality resin, craftsmen create functional 
pieces of art that are both durable and visually stunning.  
 One-of-a-Kind Aesthetics: Because no two olive wood slabs are identical, every item-from cutting boards to mezuzahs-is a unique masterpiece. The resin follows the "Live Edge" (natural contour) of the wood, ensuring no two pieces are ever the same.

Our Craft

Where Nature Meets Artistry

Every piece we create begins with a reverence for natural materials — chosen, shaped, and finished entirely by hand. This is handmade craft at its most intentional.

Handpicked Natural Hardwoods

We source only the finest olive and acacia wood — each plank chosen for its unique grain character, natural colour variation, and lasting durability. No two pieces are ever alike, because no two trees are alike.

Artful Resin Fusion

Our signature river-blue epoxy resin is hand-poured into natural wood channels, creating luminous veins of colour that mirror flowing water. Each pour is carefully timed, layered, and cured to achieve a glass-smooth, food-safe finish that endures for generations.

Meaningful Handcrafted Finishing

Every edge is hand-sanded through progressive grits. Every surface is oiled, buffed, and inspected before leaving our workshop. From natural cutting boards to handcrafted mezuzahs, the finishing is where intention becomes heirloom.

Signature Works

Each Piece Tells the Story of Its Wood

No two pieces from Natural Wood Art are alike. The grain movement of olive and acacia, the organic flow of blue epoxy resin rivers, and the quiet decisions made during hand-finishing — together they make every cutting board, mezuzah, and decorative object a singular artifact. These are not mass-produced goods; they are handmade gifts shaped by the material itself.
